PAST COIN ISSUES


50 Valora Coin



The Fifty Valora Coin was the first metal coin created by the Bank of Molossia. The coin was nickel-plated, with a milled edge and raised relief designs on the obverse and reverse. The obverse showed the Lesser Arms of Molossia, the value and the National Motto, "Nothing Ventured, Nothing Gained". The reverse commemorated the Treaty of Friendship between Molossia and Westarctica, with images of each nation's flag or arms shown, as well as the treaty date (2005) and the coin date (2006).

Note: The Fifty Valora Coin is no longer for sale.


Presidential Wedding Commemorative Coin



The Presidential Wedding Commemorative Coin was minted to commemorate the wedding of His Excellency, The President and The First Lady on 10 September 2011, XXXIV. Its face shows The President and First Lady in profile. The reverse is a composite of the initials of our nation "RM" superimposed over the stylized V?v? of Erzulie Freda, the voodoo loa (goddess) of love.
