ORIGINIA

Originia is the first state to be created by Rusha. There is only one island of Originia, but the state includes more, mostly of the Northern Vythulic Archipelago. The Rushans first landed on this original island after fourteen years in the sea.

Created: September 29th 1868

VYTHULIA

Vythulia hosts the some of the best waterscapes and forests in the entire nation. Fourty-six percent of the national parks and forests are in this wonderous state. This state was founded after going around Selakia back when it was still a sovereign nation.

MOSLANDIA

Moslandia is home to the capital city of Mosbull. This state is the smallest state yet, consisting of two small islands and one big city. Grotan claimed that this state would have the lowest population, but we now know it has the highest population. Also, the Great Game happened here.

Founded: February 28th, 1887

Capital City: Mosbull
