The Rushan Expeditions

This page will show you the timeline of how the Rushans claimed their land.

1868

On the year of 1868, the island of Originia was founded. It became the capital civilization of the small country of Rusha. These Rushans did not know yet about the mainland, which was currently riddled with the native Lanxans.

1870

On the year of 1870, the island of Ivaland and the rest of the Vythulic Archipelago was founded. Later, Ivaland would host the city of St. Petrisburg

1876

On the year of 1876, the northwest part of what is now called McDomain was founded. The Lanxans were first spotted around this time.

1881

On the year of 1881, the Rushans aquired the rest of McDomain, and founded the cities of Novgrodia and Jonebergrad. The Rushan civilization was growing very quickly.
